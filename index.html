Our Vision

Choose The Best For You

How many times have you sent an urgent email to a professor and the answer came much later because his mail was too full ? Or youâ€™re simply tired to walk through all the entire Cittadella to reach him, just to find out that ten more people had the same idea in that same moment !!!

Now you won't have these kind of problems anymore thanks to ItsYourTurn!! Real time update, notification, booking, quick information, all at your disposal. Even the room will become smart to allow you reaching the Prof faster and easyier. And all of this always under the supervision of the Professor who will interact with the system to get the best out of it and the stress out of him.

The system will recognize whether the Professor is or is not in his office automatically updating the availability state. Key informations such as phone number, e-mail and office location will be accessible (phone, web). You'll be able to book a consulence and see if there is a queue or not. If asked, a notification will inform you when the Prof becomes available and it will tell the Professor when someone is asking for him.

The professor will also have the means to change his state (free, occupied, only emergency), or even to switch off the system. He can organize ( daily, weekly, monthly) his office schedule and the system will automatically handle emergencies, conferences or other events based on the calendar.
